About

Purpose

I run into wacky, interesting, and confusing things working within the InfoSec industry. Most of the time I don’t know what I’m doing and am scared. Hopefully I can share my trials and tribulations with others through this blog. After all, the best way to learn yourself is to teach others!

About Me

I’ve spent the last 6 years working in cybersecurity operations. What does that mean? Hats…lots and lots of hats. Spend enough time in this field and you’ll realize just how much information there is to learn.

I have a particular interest working in the following areas of cybersecurity but am always learning new things.

  • SIEM Configuration & Analysis
  • Malware Analysis
  • Application Security

You might be asking yourself, “Why the donkey?” I figured it was fitting since I’m no stranger making an ass of myself when learning things.
